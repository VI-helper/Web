About Me

I am Aditya Tiwari, a school student from UtterPradesh who believes that digital knowledge should be for everyone – whether fully sighted, partially sighted, or visually impaired. My goal is to create accessible, affordable, and inclusive learning opportunities so that every individual can walk together in the digital world with independence and confidence.

My Mission

  • To spread digital literacy across all communities.
  • To provide accessible content for visually impaired learners.
  • To offer free and affordable online courses.
  • To help people become independent in the digital era.
